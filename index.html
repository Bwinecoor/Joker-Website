Origin:

The Joker, otherwise known as Jack Oswald White is a fictional character from DC Comics and is known for being a psychopathic murderer who does not care for anyone he has killed and being the arch nemesis of Batman. The creation of the Joker was inspired by the look of performer Conrad Veidt in a 1928 film adaptation of The Man Who Laughs. In the fictional world/DC Comics, most adaptations of the Joker's origin include him being a struggling comedian with a pregnant wife. He turns to a little bit of crime to get more money to support his family but during a break in at Ace Chemicals where he was disguised as Red Hood, but Batman showed up thinking he was actually the Red Hood and scared him to the point where Jack fell of the ledge and fell into a vat of acid, turning his skin white, hair green, and made him go insane.
